Improving the UI

I create all my notes on notion.so in dark mode and format them all according.

Notion has been my absolute favorite text editing tool so far and offers some really nice shortcuts that I really enjoy using.

Notion allows you to:

  1. Share public links to websites that display your content identical to the environment you edited them in.

    This is almost perfect for our use-case, but ideally I’d like to own and host the content myself.

  1. Download your content as HTML files.

    This is perfect for our use-case but the right CSS gets lost when doing so.

    I don’t know why the Notion team decided to actively downgrade the look of their HTML exports.

    This is what I wish the home page would look like (but it is not hosted by me): https://sueszli.notion.site/TU-Wien-Course-Summaries-38bf600506864f1087c3d90db5594db0

  1. Download your content as markdown files.

    This is completely useless for our use-case.

To resolve this problem we have 2 possible options:

  1. Loconotion

    The “loconotion” scraper is the best one there is out there, but it has the limitation that it doesn’t download any files.

    I opened up this issue but the developer has not responded yet: https://github.com/leoncvlt/loconotion/issues

    This is an awesome application, but it isn’t under active development any longer and you could really help me out by helping me fork and improve it because I would really love to use it more frequently.

  1. Direct download

    Alternatively we could just style the pages ourself, but I haven’t managed to do so.
